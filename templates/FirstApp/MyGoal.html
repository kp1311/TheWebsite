The goals checklist:(on this site we are primarily focused on career goals right now.)
  1. I have a clear ,specific goal.
  2. I have set a deadline to achieve that goal.
  3. I found out the specialized knowledge and skills that I need to achieve the goal.
  4. I have listed all the things i need to do or practice in order to achieve the goal.
  5. I have oraganized the list into a sequence of steps that I need to take in order
    to achieve my goal.
  6. I know which 20% of the things will produce 80% of the result.
  8. I have set aside a specific block of time everyday(one hour or even more) to work
    on my goal.
  9. I do something every single day in the specified time to achieve my goal.



If the answer to all above steps is Yes then soon you will meets success at
